# The Toast — For The Gents

> From the desk of The Lorekeeper

---

## What Is It?

**The Toast** is our private virtual cocktail party app. We (The Gents) host 4-8 invited guests through a cinematic, 35-40 minute evening — think Wes Anderson meets a speakeasy, but in a browser.

It's not a video call. It's a **show**.

---

## How It Works

We send guests a 4-letter code (like **SILK** or **NOIR**). They join, take a selfie, and our AI creates them a cocktail identity — alias, portrait, personality dossier. Nobody types during the party. Everything is taps, gestures, and reactions.

The evening runs through **4 acts** with a timer:

| Act | Name | Duration | Vibe |
|-----|------|----------|------|
| I | Arrivals | 5 min | Slow burn — getting settled |
| II | Warm-Up | 10 min | Cruise — finding the rhythm |
| III | Main Event | 15 min | Full cruise — the heart of the evening |
| IV | Last Call | 10 min | Wind down — closing notes |

At the end, every guest gets a personalized **Wrapped card** (like Spotify Wrapped) with their evening stats, photos, and a personal note from The Lorekeeper.

---

## What Each Gent Controls

### Keys & Cocktails (The Alchemist) 🍸
- **Shake to Mix** — sends an AI-generated cocktail to every guest
- Each cocktail has a unique name, backstory, and photorealistic image
- Guests can **Accept** (cheers!) or **Dodge** (bold move) — all tracked in their stats

### Beard & Bass (The Atmosphere) 🎵
- **Vibe Shift** — controls the energy of the room with 3 modes:
  - 🕯️ **Slow Burn** — candlelight, lo-fi jazz
  - 🌊 **Cruise** — bossa nova, warm bass
  - 🔥 **Ignition** — uptempo lounge, confident brass
- Shifting the vibe changes the visual feel for everyone instantly

### The Lorekeeper (The Architect) 📜
- **Confession** — triggers an AI-generated "have you ever..." prompt. Everyone votes YES or NO, then gets a witty verdict
- **Snap** — 3-2-1 countdown, everyone's camera fires simultaneously for a group photo
- **Next Act / Wrap It Up** — controls the evening's pace and can end it when the moment's right

All three of us have a **control panel** (slide-up overlay) accessible only to us during the party. Each Gent sees their own tab by default.

## The Cool Stuff

**Everything is AI-generated in real-time:**
- Guest portraits (vintage magazine aesthetic)
- Scene backdrops (rooftop bars, jazz lounges, etc.)
- Cocktail names, stories, and images
- Confession prompts that get better as the evening goes
- Personalized Wrapped cards with Lorekeeper's Note

**Zero text input during the party:**
- Guests interact only through taps, swipes, camera, and emoji reactions (🔥 ❤️ 😂 🥂 👀 💀)
- Keeps the energy flowing, no awkward typing pauses

**The Wrapped Card:**
- Every guest gets one at the end
- Shows: their AI portrait, cocktail alias, stats (drinks accepted/dodged, confessions, snaps), and a 2-sentence personal note from The Lorekeeper
- Shareable via phone's native share sheet — designed for Instagram stories
